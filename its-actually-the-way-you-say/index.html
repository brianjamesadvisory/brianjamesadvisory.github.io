It's Actually The Way You Say

April 13, 2018 in Food For Thought

Brothers,

In communication, especially with women, the gesture and the body language are much more important than the thing we say. Many, many times more. Do you know that, in reality, body language comprises up to 93% of the communication power, while the message is just little 7%.

Yes, that is true. Body language is the key factor deciding a winner or loser, not the message my friend.

So, what does body language comprise of?

  • Eye contact

  • The way you say it

  • Your body gesture

Men usually only care about what they say, the meaning, the logic, the message. They worry about being stupid when they open up. Just as, whether it sounds stupid, or misunderstanding, or maybe the other persons would take it wrongly.

Let me tell you the truth: just the message alone, it cannot influence or win the minds of women.

The more important thing, that is the way you convey that message.

In reality, women never listen to your words fully, but they pay lots of attention to the signals from your body. Which we usually call body language.

Tons of men do not know this, hence they spend endless time doing the useless things. They take days and months brainstorming about the smartest words or the most precise ones. However, they keep being disappointed because after all, women never pay attention to what they say.

Nevertheless, body language is different. By good gestures and confident look, whatever you say, it is still very attractive and impactful, deeply in her mind and her heart.

Think about an old-school boring TV comedy show. You listen them talk and talk, til so headache but cannot laugh anything out loud. However, watch such shows as Mr Bean, or Charlie Chaplin, you never hear them voice anything, but they are really fun and attractive. People like those really much.

Hence I really wish you to shift your focus to your body language rather than the message.

Trust me, that is the number 1 success recipe in dating. And by the way, I will reveal some of the important things for you to improve your body language:

  • Laugh cheerfully.

  • When you are in a conversation, lean backwards a little bit. Don’t shift yourself towards her closely.

  • Make slow and firm movements.

  • Maintain eye contact.

  • Keep your hands out of the pocket.

  • Don’t show nervousness when talking.

  • Don’t move or look around when talking.

  • Breathe fully and slowly when talking.

  • Speak slowly and clearly, no rushing.

  • When talking, spread your legs to the width of your shoulders.

  • Lift up your chest a little.

  • Lift your head up and bright.

Those are some of the basic tips for gestures and body language. Once you are familiar with them, women will respond to you with a great positiveness.

Your friend,

Brian.
