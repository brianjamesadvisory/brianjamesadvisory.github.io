Honesty Is The Sexiest Charm

April 9, 2018 in Food For Thought

Oh dear,

In previous sessions, we have discussed about some fundamental mistakes guys usually make on their journeys to win the ladies of their lives. How about today? I will be talking about Honesty. The Honesty of Men.

Well, what makes you think about honesty? Currently how many definitions are there in your own dictionary? Where does honesty stand in your life’s priority?

To me, honesty is the key that enables me successes with ladies. I have been and will always be keeping my integrity to the utmost priority in order to succeed in love. Do you believe so?

I definitely think you do. Why? Because if you are reading these lines, that means you trust me and as well as my knowledge and experiences. In other words, my strategies would richly benefit you in many ways.

So, what does honesty mean?

At first, it made me think of a clean and clear mirror. Whatever one can see through that mirror is exactly what they can see in your soul. You are true to yourself. You have nothing to hide. You are straightforward. You are trust-worthy. And you stand proud.

You are brave to do whatever it takes to achieve your dreams. You show ladies all your strengths, but never hide your weaknesses. You might think, it is even better showing your true self rather than waiting for the perfection to come. However as I always say there is no perfect man on earth. All of us are looking for a missing piece to mend our broken parts in life. But it does not mean that you keep on searching for those 1 or 2 pieces and forget about hundreds of other potential pieces which are already residing in the secret corners of your soul.

So, why does honesty attract women?

That is because, it is one of a man’s characters that makes women have the feelings of security. If she thinks that you are honest and straightforward, she will quickly trust you. After trusting you, she will open herself to you, spiritually and, physically. So if in another way, if you are trying to cheat by “acting”, or trying to hide your true self, she will not lay her trust on you. In other words, she will close tightly her feelings, and of course, her arms to you. Frankly speaking.

Now let’s think about the 2 persons whom you appear to know them the most. One of them always lies and has dishonest acts. The other one is an honest and straightforward man, even though he is not perfect. Who will you trust more? Who you will avoid?

I guess you should already have your own answer. Every single one of us likes the honest and sincere person, even if they are not perfect. Even more, the liar also likes the honest but not the dishonest - someone like himself. Since the honesty is the first foundation, the cornerstone of building trust. And definitely, women are always attracted to the real men, not the perfect ones.

So, you should confidently be yourself. Be confident in showing your true colors even though you lack of so many things. As long as women can see the positivity in your soul, she will take it more easily in accepting you, loving you for you.

Therefore, my friend, let’s be honest, and live life to the fullest.

Your friend,
Brian
